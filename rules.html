Server Rules

Nexus Gaming & Café Server Rules

This community is built for gamers and chill seekers alike.
Before you dive in, please take a moment to read through our server rules and guidelines.

All members are required to follow the rules listed below while participating in this Discord server.
Nexus Gaming & Café operates in full compliance with the Discord Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

For more details, please visit:
• Discord Community Guidelines
• Discord Terms of Service

Any situations not explicitly covered by these rules will be handled on a case-by-case basis.
Moderators reserve the right to take appropriate action when necessary, even if a specific rule does not address the issue.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or need to report something:
• Create a ticket in #request-assistance
• DM @Admin or anyone with the @Moderator / @Admin role

• Official warnings will only be issued by @NGC Bot.
• You may also receive automated messages from moderation bots about your conduct - these are informational only and do not count as official warnings.
• Please do not block these bots or disable their DMs, as they provide important updates regarding server moderation.
• In most cases, users will receive at least one mute before a ban is considered, unless the situation is severe.

Reporting & Complaints

If you witness behavior that violates the rules or poses a serious threat, report it immediately using #request-assistance or DM a mod/admin, preferably with evidence.

If you have complaints about mods, reach out to @Admin or anyone in the @Moderator / @Admin role.

Important Notes

Staff members are not exempt from server rules and regulations; they are held to the same standards as all users.

All reports and moderation actions will be handled at the discretion of the staff, in alignment with the server's rules and Discord's Community Guidelines.

Nexus Gaming & Café Discord server is a general-audience community. All content must remain appropriate for users aged 13 and older, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
