NGC Bot - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data when you use the NGC Bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic Server Information: When you add the NGC Bot to your server, we collect basic information about your server, such as the server name, ID, and member count.
  • Command Usage: We track the commands used with the bot for performance and feature improvement purposes.
  • User Interactions: Information such as user actions (e.g., bans, kicks, warnings, etc.) and interactions with the bot will be collected to provide moderation and utility features.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We do not collect any sensitive personal information unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., server description, nickname changes).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We use the collected data to enhance the functionality of the bot, improve user experience, and ensure proper moderation within your server.
  • The data collected is not shared with third parties unless required by law or for the purpose of maintaining and improving the bot.
  • Data is only stored for as long as necessary to fulfill the bot's purpose and will be deleted if the bot is removed from your server.

3. Data Security

  • We use encryption and secure protocols to protect the data stored and transmitted by the bot.
  • We take reasonable steps to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

4. User Rights

  • You have the right to request the removal of your data from the bot at any time. If you remove the bot from your server, all associated data will be deleted.
  • If you have any concerns or requests regarding your data, please contact the bot's administrator.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • It's your responsibility to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we're protecting your data.
